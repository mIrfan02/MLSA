What We Do

We are a dynamic, hyper focused community supported by Microsoft mainly operating in the Islamabad and Rawalpindi region. Our mission is to empower the masses through advanced technological education and encourage innovation.

Microsoft Technologies

We have highly specialized team which aims to advocate and instruct the latest tech from Microsoft in Web, Mobile, Design and AI.

Open Source Technologies

Open Source Technology is now the fastest way new products get built. We are strong advocates and instruct on the industry tools such GitHub.

Domains

We train and empower students for diverse careers.

Web Dev

We help students learn and build industry-grade websites using the latest technologies out there ranging from JavaScript to Cross-Platform frameworks.

Design

We help students learn the essence of design in the products of the future and help them adopt design thinking and best practices in product development.

Artificial Intelligence

We help students learn artificial intelligence by instructing natural language processing, reinforcement learning, deep neural networks, and OpenCV.

Azure Cloud

We help students learn Azure cloud computing foundations. Methods for managing a cloud computing project and designing a cloud computing infrastructure

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What services do you offer?

    We provide training and sessions on the latest technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Low Code Development, Web Development, and much more. This further extends to even career-based sessions where we invite trainers and industry experts from across the globe.

  • Are you limited to technology only?

    We are a diverse community focused on empowering the masses. Therefore, we focus on non-technical events as well that shape students into the next impact makers.

  • Is this community supported by Microsoft?

    Yes, as this community comes within the Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador program the community consists of verified MLSA’s that are overseen by Microsoft Community Program Managers.

  • How can I be a part of this community?

    You can scroll down to "Wish To Join MLSA Islamabad" and click on Register. You will be directed to a Microsoft Form where you can provide your details and register your interest in joining our community.

  • Is this community limited to students only?

    Yes, you must be a student from an accredited university.

  • Can non-technical students join MLSA Islamabad?

    As we focus on encouraging more students into technology and innovation, it truly does not matter what your discipline is. Hence, students from all respective fields are welcomed. However, we encourage some minor knowledge about technology.
